    I am a postdoc researcher in the DEEM Lab research group headed by Sebastian Schelter at Technische Universität Berlin and the BIFOLD research center in Berlin, Germany. I received my PhD from TU Berlin, where I was a research associate in the DAMS Lab group headed by Matthias Boehm. Prior to my PhD, I was a Senior Software Engineer at Teradata Labs, Hyderabad, India.

    My research interest lies broadly in the intersection of Data Management, Machine Learning and Systems. Currently, I am exploring different aspects of the ML system internals to address high computational redundancy across heterogeneous runtimes. I am also interested in large-scale ML, distributed data platforms, and transaction processing. In Teradata, I was involved in the design and development of various features inside Teradata database's execution engine.

    I am a PMC member of Apache SystemDS, which is an open source ML system for the end-to-end data science lifecycle from data integration, cleaning, and preparation, over local, distributed, and federated ML model training, to debugging and serving.
